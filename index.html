Cappuccino

Home

Diagram

Diagram goes here

Recipe

  • 1⁄3 Espresso
  • 1⁄3 Steamed Milk
  • 1⁄3 Milk Foam
  1. Pull espresso shots and pour into cup.
  2. Steam milk while ensuring maximum foamage.
  3. Pour milk & foam mixture into cup.

This drink is usually served in a small porcelain cup.
Up for a challenge? Try adding a bit of flair to your coffee-making and create artwork in the milk foam.

Coffee

Home

Video

Recipe

  • 1cup:2tbsp Ratio of Hot Water to Ground Coffee
  1. Grind coffee beans (if whole beans are purchased).
  2. Scoop the appropriate amount of grounds into the press
  3. Heat the appropriate amount of water.
  4. Pour into french press.
  5. Let steep for 1 – 2 minutes.
  6. Press grounds to the bottom
  7. Pour coffee into mug.

  • French Press
